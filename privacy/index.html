歡迎使用 smartkidsgame.com（「本網站」）。本網站提供免費嘅兒童教育小遊戲，適合小朋友喺家長陪同下遊玩。我哋非常重視私隱，尤其係兒童嘅私隱。本政策說明我哋點樣處理資料。

Welcome to smartkidsgame.com ("the Site"). The Site offers free educational mini-games for children to play with adult supervision. We take privacy seriously, especially children's privacy. This policy explains how we handle information.

1. 我哋收集嘅資料

本網站唔會收集任何個人資料。冇帳戶、冇登入、唔需要姓名或電郵。所有遊戲完全喺你嘅瀏覽器內運行。我哋只會用瀏覽器嘅「本機儲存 (local storage)」記住遊玩時間（用嚟做護眼休息提示），呢啲資料只留喺你部裝置，我哋睇唔到。

1. Information We Collect

The Site does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and no name or email required. All games run entirely in your browser. We only use your browser's local storage to remember play time (for the eye-rest reminder); this stays on your device and is not accessible to us.

2. Cookie 與廣告

本網站使用 Google AdSense 喺首頁顯示廣告。Google 及其合作夥伴可能會使用 cookie 來投放廣告。由於本網站係兒童導向，我哋已設定只顯示非個人化廣告 (non-personalized ads)，唔會根據行為追蹤投放。

你可以喺 Google 廣告政策 進一步了解 Google 點樣使用資料。

2. Cookies & Advertising

The Site uses Google AdSense to display ads on the home page. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads. Because the Site is child-directed, we have configured ads to be non-personalized only, with no behavioral tracking. Learn more at Google's Advertising Policies.

3. 兒童私隱

本網站專為兒童而設，建議喺家長陪同下使用。我哋唔會故意收集兒童嘅個人資料。為符合《兒童網上私隱保護法》(COPPA) 及相關規定，本網站只顯示非個人化廣告。

3. Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for children, ideally with adult supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. In line with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar rules, the Site serves non-personalized ads only.

4. 歐洲用戶 (GDPR)

對於歐洲經濟區 (EEA)、英國及瑞士嘅用戶，我哋採用 Google 認證嘅同意聲明管理平台 (CMP) 嚟收集 cookie 同意聲明。

4. European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK and Switzerland, we use a Google-certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) to obtain consent for cookies.

5. 政策更新

我哋可能會不時更新本私隱政策，更新會喺呢一頁公布。

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
